Records

  • Virat Kohli holds the record for the most centuries in ODI cricket by an Indian player.
  • Anil Kumble's 10/74 against Pakistan in 1999 is the best bowling figures in a Test innings by an Indian bowler.
  • Sachin Tendulkar has the most runs in international cricket.
  • Mithali Raj is the highest run-scorer in women's ODIs.
  • Rohit Sharma has the highest individual score in an ODI match (264 runs).
  • Anil Kumble has the most Test wickets by an Indian bowler.
  • M.S. Dhoni holds the record for the most international stumpings by a wicketkeeper.
  • Sunil Gavaskar was the first batsman to score 10,000 runs in Test cricket.
